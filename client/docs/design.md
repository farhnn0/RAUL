# RAUL Design System

## Brand

RAUL is a movie rating and review platform.

The experience should feel:

* Cinematic
* Professional
* Modern
* Editorial
* Community-driven

Avoid:

* SaaS aesthetics
* AI-generated aesthetics
* Neon effects
* Glassmorphism
* Gradients
* Purple accents

* Fade
* Scale

* Bounce

## Design Principle

Movie content is always more important than decorative UI elements.

Posters, ratings, reviews, and movie information should be the primary focus.
